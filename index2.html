FAQ

A quick overview of how Asta works, what it’s for, and what to expect.

What is Asta? +
Asta is a relationship companion that helps you understand connection through reflection, communication, and clear signals — so you can move forward with intention.
Is Asta for dating, relationships, or both? +
Both. Asta is designed for anyone navigating connection — meeting someone new, deepening a bond, rebuilding trust, or deciding to let go.
How do “connection signals” work? +
Signals are visual summaries that help you notice patterns — like clarity, stability, or momentum. They’re meant to support reflection and better conversations, not replace human judgment.
Do you host community events? +
Yes. Asta events are designed to bring online insights into offline experiences — real conversations, real connection, and shared learning.
How do I get access? +
Join the community to get early updates and access announcements. You can also request beta access via the CTA buttons above.
